Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

1. Data We Collect

When you create an account, we collect your name and email address. When you use Spendly, we collect the expense data you enter — including amounts, categories, dates, and descriptions. We do not collect payment information, location data, or any data beyond what you explicitly provide.

2. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used solely to provide the Spendly service — to display your expense history, generate summaries, and personalise your experience. We do not use your data for advertising, profiling, or any purpose unrelated to the service.

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored securely. We take reasonable technical measures to protect your information from unauthorised access, loss, or disclosure. You can access, edit, or delete any expense data you have entered at any time within the app. You may also request deletion of your account and all associated data by contacting us.

4. Third Party Services

Spendly does not sell, rent, or share your personal or financial data with third parties. We do not integrate with any external analytics, advertising, or tracking services. We may disclose information only if required to do so by law or in response to a valid legal request.

5. Contact Us

If you have questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or how your data is handled, please reach out through the app. We will do our best to respond promptly.
